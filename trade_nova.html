Welcome to the NOVA trading portal! Here, you can learn how to purchase NOVA in a way that unlocks exclusive rewards, ensuring you maximize your benefits while contributing to our vibrant digital economy.

1. Contract Functionality Overview

The NOVA smart contract offers the following primary functions to facilitate transparent and trackable transactions:

  • Buy (`buy`): Purchase NOVA using SOL and receive additional NOVA rewards based on our dynamic reward mechanism.
  • Sell (`sell`): Sell NOVA back to the contract in exchange for SOL.
  • Transfer NOVA (`transfer_nova`): Transfer NOVA between users securely without incurring transaction fees.

2. How to Buy NOVA

Purchase Method: `buy`

**Functionality:**

  • Use this method to buy NOVA with SOL.
  • The method automatically calculates and distributes additional NOVA rewards based on the current transaction phase.
  • A 5% fee is applied to each purchase, which supports the developer reward pool and liquidity pool maintenance.

**Parameters:**

  • `sol_amount`: The amount of SOL you wish to spend (minimum of 0.0001 SOL).
  • `current_nova_price`: The current price per NOVA token, typically provided by the contract or user interface.

**Execution Process:**

  1. **Transfer SOL to Liquidity Pool PDA:**
    • Send the specified amount of SOL from your wallet to the contract's Liquidity Pool PDA address.
  2. **Receive NOVA Tokens:**
    • Based on the current NOVA price and reward percentage, receive the corresponding amount of NOVA tokens in your wallet.
  3. **Fee Deduction:**
    • A 5% fee is deducted from your purchase amount and allocated to the Developer Reward Pool to support ongoing development and liquidity.

3. How to Sell NOVA

Sell Method: `sell`

**Functionality:**

  • Sell your NOVA tokens back to the contract in exchange for SOL.
  • A 5% fee is applied to each sale, supporting liquidity pool maintenance.

**Parameters:**

  • `nova_amount`: The amount of NOVA you wish to sell.
  • `current_nova_price`: The current price per NOVA token, typically provided by the contract or user interface.

**Execution Process:**

  1. **Transfer NOVA to Treasury:**
    • Send the specified amount of NOVA from your wallet to the contract's Treasury address.
  2. **Receive SOL:**
    • Based on the current NOVA price, receive the corresponding amount of SOL in your wallet after a 5% fee deduction.
  3. **Fee Deduction:**
    • A 5% fee is deducted from your sale amount and allocated to the Liquidity Pool PDA to support liquidity.

4. How to Transfer NOVA

Transfer Method: `transfer_nova`

**Functionality:**

  • Transfer NOVA tokens between users securely without any transaction fees.

**Parameters:**

  • `from`: The sender's wallet address (must sign the transaction).
  • `from_nova_account`: The sender's NOVA Token Account.
  • `to_nova_account`: The recipient's NOVA Token Account.
  • `amount`: The amount of NOVA tokens to transfer.

**Execution Process:**

  1. **Verify Balance:**
    • Ensure the sender has sufficient NOVA balance to cover the transfer amount.
  2. **Execute Transfer:**
    • Initiate the transfer of the specified NOVA amount from the sender's account to the recipient's account.

5. Important Considerations

  • Reward Mechanism Activation: Rewards are only triggered when purchasing NOVA through the contract's `buy` method. Purchasing via other methods, such as decentralized exchanges (DEX), will not activate the reward distribution.
  • Fee Allocation: A 5% fee is applied to all buy and sell transactions. These fees support the Developer Reward Pool and Liquidity Pool to maintain system stability and ongoing development.
  • Minimum Transaction Limits:
    • Buy: Minimum of 0.0001 SOL per transaction.
    • Sell: Must have a sufficient NOVA balance to cover the sale amount.
